3Wood Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

What we collect

  • Account information: your email address and chosen username.
  • Your activity: the golf courses you rank, your scores, want-to-play bookmarks, and who you follow.

How it's used

  • Your rankings power your personal course list and the community average ratings shown to other users.
  • Your username, rankings, and lists are visible to other signed-in 3Wood users — that's the social heart of the app.
  • Your email address is never shown to other users and is used only for sign-in.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • We don't collect your location. The map shows courses, not you.

Data storage

Data is stored with Supabase (hosted PostgreSQL) in the United States.

Deleting your account

In the app: Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, rankings, bookmarks, and follows. You can also email us to request deletion.
