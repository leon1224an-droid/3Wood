Using 3Wood

3Wood lets you rank golf courses you've played, keep a want-to-play list, write course reviews, and follow other golfers. You need an account to use the app, and you're responsible for what you post from it.

Your content

You own the reviews and activity you post. By posting, you give 3Wood permission to display them to other users — that's the point of the app. Course rankings, usernames, and reviews are visible to all signed-in users.

Objectionable content is not tolerated

There is zero tolerance for objectionable content or abusive behavior: harassment, hate speech, spam, impersonation, or anything unlawful. You can report any review or user from inside the app, and block any user to hide their content from you. We review reports within 24 hours and remove violating content and, where warranted, the accounts that posted it.

Your account

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete account, which permanently removes your profile, rankings, lists, and reviews.
